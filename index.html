Our Ministries

Our various Discipleship programs includes:

Discipleship trainings

Empowering believers through structured teachings and mentorship programs designed to deepen their understanding of the Gospel and equip them for ministry.

Campus Coordinators training

Providing leadership training and spiritual guidance to campus coordinators, empowering them to effectively lead and disciple students in their respective campuses.

Mentorship academy / Power Bible school

Providing in-depth biblical education and mentorship to equip believers with the knowledge and skills needed for effective ministry and personal spiritual growth.

school of ministry

Providing specialized training and resources for individuals called to ministry, equipping them with the tools and knowledge to effectively serve and lead in their communities.
